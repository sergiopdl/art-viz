To help you plan your visit to the museum, use the following tool to explore the artworks on view.

Use the map on the left to choose a gallery.

Artworks are grouped by artist, decreasing by iconicity (clockwise).

In the center, galleries are ordered according to the same layout as the map (clockwise).

Links from artworks to galleries indicate related works in those galleries.

Hover over artworks or galleries to isolate relationships with other artworks.

On the right, you can see images of the selected work and its related works.
