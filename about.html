About

The purpose of this website is to help you plan your visit to the National Gallery of Art in Washington, D.C.

Information about artworks, including iconicity values and related works, was downloaded from the Artsy API.

Information about artworks on view was collected from the National Gallery of Art website.

This website was developed for the course SI 649/EECS 548 (Information Visualization) at the University of Michigan.

Visualizations were built using D3, and the site was styled using Bootstrap.

This site is not officially affiliated with Artsy nor the National Gallery of Art.
