About

The purpose of this website is to help you plan your visit to the National Gallery of Art.

Information about artworks, including iconicity scores and related works, was collected from the Artsy API.

Information about which artworks are on view was collected from the National Gallery of Art website.

This website was developed for the course SI 649/EECS 548 (Information Visualization) at the University of Michigan.

The visualizations were built using D3, and the site was styled using Bootstrap.

Our work is not officially affiliated with Artsy nor the National Gallery of Art.
